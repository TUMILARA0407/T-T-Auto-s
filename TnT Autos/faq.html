Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and vehicles

What types of vehicles do you sell? +
We offer a wide selection of quality vehicles including sedans, SUVs, and compact cars. All our vehicles are inspected and certified to meet our high standards before sale. Visit our inventory page to browse our current selection.
Do you provide financing options? +
Yes, we work with multiple lenders to provide flexible financing solutions tailored to your needs. Our team can help you explore various payment plans and options that fit your budget.
Are your vehicles inspected before sale? +
Absolutely! Every vehicle undergoes thorough mechanical and safety inspections. We ensure that each car meets our strict quality standards and is road-ready before it reaches our customers.
What is your warranty policy? +
We offer warranty coverage on selected vehicles. The details vary depending on the vehicle and its age. Please contact us for specific warranty information on the vehicle you're interested in.
How can I contact you for more information? +
You can reach us via email, phone, or visit us in person at Babcock University main campus in Ilisan-Remo, Ogun State. Check our contact page for specific details and our team's contact information.
What is your after-sales support like? +
Our relationship doesn't end at the sale! We provide ongoing support, maintenance guidance, and assistance. Our team is always available to help with any questions or concerns about your vehicle.
